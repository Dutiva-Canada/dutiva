What’s the minimum notice for termination in Ontario?
For most Ontario employees, statutory notice depends on length of service under the ESA. For an employee with 3 years of service, the minimum is 3 weeks’ notice or pay in lieu.
Common law notice or severance obligations may be higher depending on the circumstances.
